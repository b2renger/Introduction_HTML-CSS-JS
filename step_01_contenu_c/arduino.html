Arduino is a fun way to approach electronics. For anyone willing to control physical things, or gather data about his environment it's probably the go to project you'll need.

Arduino is modular by essence. You can plug many kinds of components : ranging from lunminosity sensors and buttons to led and servos, including cameras, screens etc.

This little gif is a demonstration of analog in -> digital out. Arduino is used to gather luminosity data and transfers it via USB to a processing programm drawing a tree. The span of the tree is directly linked to the ambiant luminosity.

There are a lot of third party library and components. For instance 1Sheeld. Allows you to easilty interact with your phone via its companion app. It is the case in this small text-to-speech example.
